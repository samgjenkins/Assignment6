Don't Falter - Chat with Walter

 

Breaking Bad is an American crime drama television series created and produced by Vince Gilligan. The show aired from January 2008 to September 2013.

Walter White is the protagonist of the series. Portrayed by actor Bryan Cranston, the character embodies a high school chemistry teacher diagnosed with lung cancer who turns to cooking and selling meth in order to provide for his family.

The show received numerous awards and is well on its way to becoming recognized as classic TV. It has given rise to a sequel movie and a follow-up television series, Better Call Saul.
